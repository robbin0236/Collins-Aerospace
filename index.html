How to Update Collins Aerospace GPS Map?

Collins Aerospace GPS systems play a critical role in global aviation navigation. Regularly updating your GPS database ensures compliance with global airspace changes and enhances in-flight safety and performance.

Why Update Collins Aerospace Maps?

  • Stay current with AIRAC navigation cycles
  • Access new waypoints, flight paths, and airspace boundaries
  • Optimize route planning and fuel efficiency
  • Ensure compatibility with latest avionics systems

What You Need Before Updating

  • Collins Aerospace GPS-equipped aircraft or device
  • USB/SD card or compatible data transfer method
  • Access to Collins’ update portal or flight deck interface
  • Valid update subscription or licensing credentials

Steps to Update Collins Aerospace GPS Maps

1. Access the Update Platform

Go to the official Collins Aerospace website and log into your avionics portal or data services interface.

2. Select Your Equipment

Choose your aircraft model or GPS system from the available list and verify your subscription status.

3. Download Update Package

Select the latest navigation update (based on AIRAC cycle) and download the data package to your computer or USB stick.

4. Install on GPS Unit

Connect the USB/SD to the Collins GPS unit and follow the system prompts to load the new data files securely.

5. Confirm & Validate

After reboot, check the system status and database version to ensure updates were applied successfully.

Warning: Do not power off or disconnect the GPS system during the update process. Incomplete updates can affect flight-critical functions.

Need Help?

If you run into trouble during the update:

  • Ensure your internet and power sources are stable
  • Double-check your device compatibility
  • Use the Collins Aerospace Support Chat for real-time assistance

How Often to Update?

Collins recommends updating your GPS maps with every new AIRAC cycle (typically every 28 days) for compliant and up-to-date operations.

Conclusion

Updating your Collins Aerospace GPS system is a vital task that supports safety, compliance, and efficient flight operations. Follow this guide to keep your avionics accurate and reliable for every journey.
